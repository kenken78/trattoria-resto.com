About Us

Trattoria is a modern and contemporary Italian restaurant that has a menu inspired by the coastal regions of the Mediterranean. It elevated the standards of restaurant designs in Boracay with its characteristic stylishly modern, yet tropical, interiors and cozy ambiance.
